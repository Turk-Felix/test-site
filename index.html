FAQ

Common questions,
honest answers.

Everything you need to know before getting started.

Do I need to provide the content and photos? +
Not necessarily. We can help write copy for your site and source professional stock photography. If you have your own photos or brand materials, those are always best — but it's not a requirement to get started.
What happens after launch — will I be able to update my site? +
Yes. Every site is built with an easy-to-use CMS so you can update text, photos, and pricing yourself with no coding knowledge. We also provide a short walkthrough video so you feel confident managing things.
What do hosting and domain cost? +
Hosting typically costs €8–15/month depending on the plan, and a domain is around €12–20/year. These are billed to you directly — there's no markup from our end. We'll guide you through setting everything up.
Do you work with businesses outside Luxembourg? +
Yes — we work remotely with clients across Europe. Being Luxembourg-based just means we're especially familiar with the local market, but geography is no barrier. All projects are managed online.
